Who We Are

20+ Years of Trusted Moving in Kolkata

R K Packers & Movers is a Kolkata-based relocation company built on one simple promise — to move your belongings as carefully as if they were our own. For over 20 years we have helped thousands of families and businesses relocate safely across Kolkata and all over India.

From delicate glassware to heavy furniture, from a studio apartment to a full corporate office, our trained team handles every move with quality packing materials, well-maintained vehicles and honest, transparent pricing.

  • Licensed & experienced moving company
  • In-house trained packing & handling staff
  • Pan-India network with GPS-tracked vehicles
