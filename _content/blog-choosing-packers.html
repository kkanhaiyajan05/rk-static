How to Choose the Right Packers and Movers in Kolkata

Home›Blog›Choosing Packers

Guide · 6 min read

Choosing a moving company is one of the most important decisions you'll make during a relocation. The right partner makes your move smooth and stress-free; the wrong one can lead to damaged goods, hidden charges and endless headaches. Here's how to pick a reliable packers and movers in Kolkata.

1. Check experience and reputation

Look for a company with several years of proven experience and genuine customer reviews on Google. Established movers have refined processes, trained staff and the right equipment. Read both the positive and negative reviews to understand how they handle real situations.

2. Verify credentials

A trustworthy mover should have a proper office address, GST registration and verifiable contact details. Avoid companies that only operate through a phone number with no physical presence.

3. Insist on a written, itemised quote

Never rely on a rough figure over the phone. A professional company will do a survey (in person or over video) and provide a written, itemised quotation covering packing materials, labour, transport, taxes and insurance. This protects you from surprise charges on moving day.

4. Ask about packing materials and process

Quality packing is what keeps your belongings safe. Ask what materials they use — bubble wrap, foam, corrugated sheets, sturdy cartons and wooden crates for valuables — and how they handle fragile items and furniture.

5. Understand insurance coverage

For long-distance or high-value moves, transit insurance is essential. Ask what coverage is offered, what it costs and what the claim process looks like. A reputable mover will explain this clearly.

6. Watch for red flags

  • Unusually low quotes that seem too good to be true
  • Demands for a large advance payment
  • No written quote or refusal to do a survey
  • Vague answers about insurance and process

The bottom line

Take a little time to compare two or three companies on experience, transparency and reviews — not just price.
